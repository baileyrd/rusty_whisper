rusty-whisper

OpenAI Whisper speech recognition running entirely in your browser — a pure-Rust port of whisper.cpp compiled to WebAssembly. Nothing is uploaded; audio is decoded and transcribed locally.

1. Model

a ggml .bin, e.g. ggml-tiny.en-q5_1.bin from whisper.cpp on Hugging Face

2. Audio

any format your browser can decode; resampled to 16 kHz automatically
